About Barry Guthertz

Barry Guthertz has been a photographer for over 35 years, throughout the entire Northeast. His fine art and award winning work has appeared in many places, including The Brooklyn Museum, The French Embassy, and Silvermine Gallery in New Canaan, CT. His numerous exhibitions show his passion for his work/photography. Currently, he lives in Fairfield County, CT. His Black and White portfolios have been shown twice in Lenswork magazine, and once on Shadow and Light Magazine.

"Photography has become the medium to express my love for the mysterious compositions and beauty I find being in nature. When I’m connected to my photography there is a deep feeling of peace and wholeness—of being exactly where I need to be."
